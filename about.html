Our story

Dallas’s Trusted Auto Repair Experts

Our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto repair with transparency and integrity. We believe in treating every customer like family.

GBG Auto Repair & Inspection was founded in 2005 with a simple goal: offer reliable auto repair without the dealership price tag. Starting with just two bays, we quickly earned a reputation for honest diagnostics and quality work. Over the years, we’ve grown into a full-service shop serving thousands of Dallas drivers.

Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians continues that tradition. We invest in the latest tools and training to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex engine repairs. But our core values remain the same: transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a long-time customer, you’ll always get straight answers and fair prices.
