State Inspections

Quick and thorough Texas state vehicle inspections to keep you legal.

Schedule this service

Overview

We perform official Texas state inspections for all passenger vehicles. Our inspectors check safety items like lights, brakes, tires, and emissions (where required). We aim for fast turnaround so you can get back on the road.

What's included

  • Convenient, walk-in friendly service
  • Thorough inspection by certified inspectors
  • Immediate results and sticker issuance

How it works

1

Vehicle Check-In

Bring your vehicle and registration. We'll start the inspection right away.

2

Safety & Emissions Test

We test all required components including lights, brakes, horn, and emissions system.

3

Sticker Issuance

If your vehicle passes, we provide the inspection sticker and update the state database.
